Format of Query

Except for inputting a word, a phrase or a sentense, our website also supports Phrase Search, Proximity Search and Boolean Search.

1. Phrase Search

Format: "phrase"

You can search for an exact word or phrase to find specific information.

 

2. Proximity Search

Format: #(distance){term1, term2}

The number 'distance' means the distance between 'term1' and 'term2'. For example: #(10){python and java, course}.

 

3. Boolean Search

Format: #10{term1, term2} AND website OR "phrase search" AND NOT link

You can use "AND", "OR", "NOT" in the Query. Parentheses not supported yet.
